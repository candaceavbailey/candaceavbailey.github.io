Objective

Assisting Instacart’s marketing team, CareerFoundry course project, to uncover more information about their sales patterns. Performed initial data exploratory analysis of some of their data in order to derive insights and suggest strategies for better segmentation based on provided criteria.

  • Github

Goal

Present data manipulation and findings in a detailed report for stakeholders. Analyze customer spending trends and create sub categories for targeting marketing efforts.

Challenge

To derive new variables to segment customer purchasing habits in order to provide data driven suggestions for marketing efforts.

Preparation and analysis

Using Python installed pandas and numpy models then imported customer, department and product data.

Cleaned, wrangled, and checked data for consistency resolving issues with missing values and removing duplicates.

Combined data sets to explore and create new comparison variables such as shopping regions, active/ inactive customers, family status and more.

Key questions

  • What's the distribution among users in regards to brand loyalty? (how often do they return to Instacart)
  • Are there differences in ordering habits based on customer’s loyalty status?
  • Are there differences in ordering habits in a customer’s region?
  • Is there a connection between age and family status in terms of ordering habits?
  • What different classifications does demographic information suggest? Age? Income? Certain types of goods? Family status?
  • What differences can be found in ordering habits of different customer profiles?

Creating Variables & Answering Questions

Based on Instacart data created new variables to help the marketing department understand customer trends and distribution across geographic regions and other household stats.

Regions

  • North, South, Midwest, West
  • Based on this information data revealed there are more customers in the south than any other region.

Family Status

  • Customers are categorized by their family and dependant status.
  • Customer married with children made up the largest percentage of instacart customers.

Spending flag

  • Loyal customers are high spenders
  • New customers are low spenders
  • There are more high spending regular customers than low spending regular customers
